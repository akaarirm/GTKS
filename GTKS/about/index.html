History


In 1999, GTKS opened the first office in XX, Puerto Rico with only a handful of engineers and project management professionals. Over the last 16 years, the business has grown to be support clients from the West Coast to the East Coast, including Puerto Rico. GTKS transformed from solely providing project management services and has expanded to include experts in commissioning and qualification, start-up, project controls, project and construction management, and engineering/maintenance support professionals.
